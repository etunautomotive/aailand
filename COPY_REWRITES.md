Keep it simple, but make sure every quote:
1. **Names the problem** ("Leads were dying...")
2. **States the solution** ("Automotive AI started...")
3. **Shows the metric** ("...6 extra deals per month")
4. **Ends with conviction** (short, punchy final sentence)

## VOICE VERIFICATION

For each rewrite, ask:
1. **Does a real GM say this out loud?** (Conversation Test)
2. **Could this describe any other product?** (Pasta Test)
3. **Does it sound like a person, committee, or AI?** (Company Page Test)

If yes to Q1 and no to Q2-3, ship it.

## TONE LOCK

All rewrites follow these rules:
- ✅ **Specific numbers, not vague claims**
- ✅ **Named problems, not generic benefits**
- ✅ **Show what the AI does, don't label it**
- ✅ **Confidence, not hedging**
- ✅ **Sharp, not warm**
- ✅ **Dealer-specific references, not pop culture**
- ✅ **Founder credibility ("Built by closers who did $300K/month")**
- ✅ **Guarantee language in body copy, not fine print**

**NEVER:**
- ❌ Sentences that pass the Pasta Test
- ❌ "AI-powered," "AI-driven," "AI-native" labels
- ❌ Hedging: "we believe," "we strive," "in our opinion"
- ❌ Generic adjectives: "innovative," "cutting-edge," "powerful"
- ❌ Vague benefits: "helps," "improves," "enables"
- ❌ Em dashes anywhere
- ❌ Sentences nobody at your company would say out loud
